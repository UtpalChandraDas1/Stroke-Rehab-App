Project Goals

  • AI model-based web application to Stroke Rehabilitation.
  • It will help the doctors to diagnosis Stroke patients can create a suitable treatment plan and follow-up duration for the patient.
  • It will help stroke survivor to do the home rehabilitation .
  • Minimize unnecessary visits for the patient, improving healthcare facilities and reducing indirect costs.
