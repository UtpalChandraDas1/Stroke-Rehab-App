Stroke Rehabilitation Application

Project Goals

  • AI model-based web application to classify fundus images into normal, dry AMD, and wet AMD disease.
  • Based on the diagnosis results given by the proposed model, the ophthalmologist can create a suitable treatment plan and follow-up duration for the patient.
  • Minimize unnecessary visits for the patient, improving healthcare facilities and reducing indirect costs.
